About Sharedlist

What is it?

Sharedlist is a yet another simple checklist app that allows you to create and share checklists with others without signing up or logging in. You can choose between read only sharing or allow the people to modify the checklist when you share with read and write permissions.

How to use it?

Simply replace the heading with you desired checklist name and press enter on your keyboard. Please note pressing enter is the only way to enter new items in you checklist. Once you are done click save button to save the checklist. Use my checklist button to view your saved checklists. You can share your checklist by clicking the share button below the checklist. Just choose permissions and copy the link to share with others. Similarly you can delete your checklist by clicking the delete.

Important Note: you can delete or share checklists that are shared with you by other users. However, you can edit them if the other person has provide you with read and write permissions.

Word of caution

Since the app doesnt require you to sign up or log in, your anonymous id is store in your browser, hence you can only access your checklists from the browser you used initially. Also if you clear your browser data, you will lose all your checklists. So please be careful. In future I will add functionality to bookmark your checklists so you can have access to them even if you clear your browser data and across devices.

Troubleshooting

This project is stil a work in progress, so if you face any glitches, try refereshing the page. If that doesnt work, please contact me by clicking the report bug button at the top right corner.
